Foundation

The entire Christian life fully depends on Scripture:
All Scripture is God-breathed and is useful for teaching, rebuking, correcting and training in righteousness, so that the servant of God may be thoroughly equipped for every good work.
2 Tim. 3:16, 17

Necessity

Education is necessary regardless of spiritual experience:
When you come, bring the cloak that I left with Carpus at Troas, and my scrolls, especially the parchments.
2 Tim. 4:13

Hope

Education depends on God's action:
The earth is filled with your love, Lord; teach me your decrees. Do good to your servant according to your word, Lord. Teach me knowledge and good judgment, for I trust your commands.
Psalm 119:64-66

Bible Institute
«Word of Grace»

The Bible Institute 'Word of Grace' (BIWG) is part of the ministry of the Word of Grace Bible Church (WGBS) and primarily serves the goals and needs of this church. The school supports the overall church process of spiritual formation of its members and the preparation of WGBS ministers. It unites the ministry of similar schools in Russia and Ukraine. This school has been functioning at our church since 2001. Its ultimate goal is to glorify God through teaching church members the fundamental biblical disciplines, helping each believer to have a proper understanding of God's Word, bowing before God and practically applying the understood truths in all areas of personal life.

The Bible Institute 'Word of Grace' is a member of The Master’s Academy International (TMAI). Its theological position and ministry philosophy are in alignment with the positions of this organization. All disciplines taught at BIWG meet the academic standards of TMAI.
