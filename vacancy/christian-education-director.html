Christian Education Director

A position is open for a Christian Education Director (Battle Ground, WA)

Job Duties:

  • Teach and develop curriculum in hermeneutics, homiletics, and expository preaching
  • Record and manage seminars using Sony video equipment, ensuring high-quality production
  • Maintain and update material on the employer’s web-based training sites
  • Edit and produce video content using Adobe Premiere Pro and Adobe AfterEffects
  • Collaborate with other educators and IT professionals to support the overall educational mission
  • Travel to teach and meet students from other countries, at least once a year

Travel required: Traveling accress the U.S. and foreign countries at least once a year

Job Requirements

  • Education: Master’s degree in Theological Studies, Pastoral Theology, or Expository Preaching
  • Experience: 2 years of experience in teaching at Christian education institutes.
  • Language: Academic level knowledge of the Russian language for teaching and preaching
  • Other Skills:
    1. 2 years of experience in using online learning platforms such as Canvas and Moodle;
    2. 2 years of experiencing in working with contractors for building and maintaining websites;
    3. 2 years of experience in Sony video equipment and video editing programs such as Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe AfterEffects;
    4. 2 years of experience in managing a team of faculty members;
    5. Membership in Christian church

Position Info

  • Type:   Full time
  • Location:   Battle Ground, WA
  • Salary:   $108,514 / year
